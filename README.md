# volos

Modern Volos is built on the area of the ancient cities of Demetrias, Pagasae and Iolcos.
Demetrias was established in 293 BC by Demetrius Poliorcetes, King of Macedon.
Iolcus, or Iolkos, was known in mythology as the homeland of the hero Jason,
who boarded the ship Argo accompanied by the Argonauts and sailed in quest
of the Golden Fleece to Colchis. - Wikipedia
